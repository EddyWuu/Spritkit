# Privacy Policy — Spritkit

**Last updated:** April 19, 2026

## Overview

Spritkit is a sprite operations toolkit that processes images entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Photo Library Access

Spritkit requests access to your Photo Library so you can import images for processing (pixelation, scaling, palette extraction, sprite sheet cutting, and animation preview). All image processing happens locally on your device. Images are never uploaded to any server.

## Data Collection

**We collect nothing.** Specifically:

- No analytics or tracking
- No personal information
- No usage data
- No cookies
- No third-party SDKs that collect data
- No network requests of any kind

## Data Storage

All data (imported images, exported results) stays on your device in the app's sandboxed storage or your Photo Library. Nothing is transmitted externally.

## Third-Party Services

Spritkit uses no third-party services, APIs, or SDKs.

## Children's Privacy

Spritkit does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
